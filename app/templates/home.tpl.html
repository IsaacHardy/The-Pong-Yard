Welcome to The Pong Yard!

The Pong Yard is a place to challenge your foes, advance your ego, and log all of your triumphs (and possibly your follys).

How to use The Pong Yard:
  1. Add your name into the pool of players by following the link below
  2. Familarize yourself with the leaderboard by clicking the 'Leaderboard' tab on the top-right of screen.
  3. Click the Challenge tab to head to player selection.
  4. First click your name, (you'll notice your name will appear in the 'You' location) and then select your opponent.
  5. At the end of the match, log the results by selecting the victor!
